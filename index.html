About the Teacher

Teaching Chemistry in
Bahawalpur Since 2009

Sir Junaid Nizami is one of the most trusted chemistry teachers in Bahawalpur. Over the past 15 years, he has taught hundreds of students — from those struggling to pass, to those who went on to top the board exams and secure seats in top medical and engineering colleges.

His classes are based in Bahawalpur and cover the complete syllabus for both Punjab Board and Federal Board. He focuses on building real understanding, not just memorization — students learn the why behind every concept, which is why his results are consistently strong year after year.

Whether you're aiming for MDCAT, ECAT, or just want strong board marks, Sir Junaid's coaching gives you the solid chemistry foundation you need.
